✅ Review Checklist

After you get the AI output, check these:

  • Did the AI fill in environment details you didn't provide? (hallucination!)
  • Are the steps to reproduce accurate to what you described?
  • Did it correctly mark missing fields as [MISSING]?
  • Is the severity suggestion reasonable? Would you change it?
  • Did it avoid suggesting root cause?
  • Is the language neutral and technical?
  • Could you submit this as-is, or does it need editing?
